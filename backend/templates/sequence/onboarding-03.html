You've got a lead—now what? Here are our proven best practices for reaching out to couples and converting leads into bookings.

Top Tip

Text messages work best! We've found that couples respond much faster to a friendly text than to emails or phone calls. Keep it short, personal, and professional.

The Perfect First Message

What to Include

Mention You Were Referred

Start by saying you were referred by Precious Pics Production. This establishes credibility and reminds them they requested wedding vendor information.

What to Include

Send Your Portfolio Immediately

Don't wait—include a link to your best work right in your first message. Let them see what you offer before they even respond.

What to Include

Reference Their Wedding Details

Mention their wedding date or location. It shows you've done your homework and aren't sending a generic message.

Sample Text Message:

"Hi [Name]! I was referred to you by Precious Pics Production. I'm a [your service] in [location] and would love to help make your [date] wedding amazing! Here's some of my recent work: [portfolio link]. Would you like to chat about your vision?"

Follow-Up Strategy

If you don't hear back within 2-3 days, send a friendly follow-up. Couples are busy planning—a gentle reminder often does the trick. We recommend 2-3 follow-ups spaced a few days apart.
